Team VERA

Computer Vision for Animal Conservation

The Video-based Re-Identification and Behavior Analysis for Animals (VERA) project is an interdisciplinary collaboration led by the Machine Learning and Data Analytics Lab (MaD Lab). The VERA project forms a bridge between biological and ecological research and the machine learning field in order to develop a new generation of video-based algorithms for the tasks of re-identification of individual animals as well as detection of shown behaviour.
